What is Ant-i?

Ant-i is an ecological solution to all your ant problems.

Without any need for chemical substances you can have your always-wanted ant-free garden. Ant-i is just an anteater who loves his job, he helps you to get rid those pesky ants in your lawn.

Why Ant-i?

You will have a unique experience that not only helps the environment without using the pesticides that harm plantlife, but will also give you stories to tell on many occasions.

On top of that you will help us take care of Ant-i by feeding our anteater!
