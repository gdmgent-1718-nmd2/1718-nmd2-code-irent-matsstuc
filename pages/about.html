What is Ant-i?

Ant-i is an ecological solution to all your ant problems. Without any need for chemical substances you can have your always-wanted clean garden. Ant-i is basically just an anteater who loves his job, he helps you to get rid those pesky ants in your lawn.

What makes Ant-i?

Ant-i is a service that started with a strong belief in ecological solutions. We are against pesticides, poisons and believe that everything can be solved naturally. That is why we started Ant-i. By using an anteater we ensure that your garden will be in perfect condition by not ruining it with chemicals.

Why Ant-i?

You will have a unique experience that not only helps the environment without using the pesticides that harm plantlife, but will also give you stories to tell on many occasions. On top of that you will help us take care of our anteater by feeding him.
